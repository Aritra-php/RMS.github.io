About Us

RMS is an educational organization. It's a Web application which is used to showcase the results of students belonging to various schools,colleges,universities and educational institutes. This Web application is built by Mr. Aritra Ghosh and it's been on board with various education institutes for showcasing their students results for the past one year.

Privacy Policy

RMS is not hackable. Marks entered by the teachers can be changed or edited by the students but cannot be changed or edited by the students who are viewing their respective results. Students are not allowed to tamper with the marks.
